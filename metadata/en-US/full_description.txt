Ampersand is a front tracker application that helps you as a person with complex dissociative symptoms, such as DID or OSDD, to track who of your alters is in front at a given time, plus it helps you to communicate across alters.

Ampersand is an offline application, and as such, it will work without Internet connection. Since it operates offline, this app does not collect any user data.

Ampersand is not a medical product, and it does not constitute medical advice.

Ampersand's source code is available at: https://codeberg.org/Ampersand/app

Please report any and all issues over at Codeberg!
